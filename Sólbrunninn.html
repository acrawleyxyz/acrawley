Project Name: Sólbrunninn
Year: 2016 -


With the creation of homemade pinhole cameras the opportunity to manipulate how light was traversing through the camera to be caught on the film opened up. Fitting the inside of the cameras with reflective materials, two way mirrors, I was able to capture specific textures and general warping of light as it made its way towards the film.

 

Really need to make some more of these, they were fun.
