Project Name: Heiðingjar
Year: 2017 -


Although not the first series (that would be those pink mountains you can find on this website somewhere I shot in Norway) these photographs are the quintessential straight forward pinhole shots of natural wilderness.

 

Mostly from a disconnect felt between a more straight forward photographic approach and said subject, I predominately just wanted to sit around in the countryside and take long drawn out photographs. I purely indulgent series of a slower more tactile form of photography where I get to sit around outside for three to four minutes at a time and let the frame expose.
